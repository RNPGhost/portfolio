About

Sam Hutton

Portfolio

I am currently working as a Software Developer, having completed a four year Masters Degree in Computer Science at Oxford University. This combination of theoretical and industry experience has given me the skills and confidence needed to design and develop my own games, such as the FPS '2DV' and the turn based puzzle mobile game 'Lights Out'.

Ever since peering over my dad’s shoulder as he played the first Tomb Raider on PC, I’ve been fascinated by games. I now spend the vast majority of my free time playing, watching, analyzing, designing and developing games. I particularly enjoy RPGs such as 'The Elder Scrolls: Skyrim' and the Dark Souls series, as well as FPSs such as the Battlefield series.

When not gaming, I spend my time dancing, kayaking and practicing martial arts. I have competed with the Oxford University Dancesport Team in Ballroom and Latin dances as well as Salsa, and have been teaching Salsa classes for the last two years. In 2014, I took part in a 10 hour continuous dance marathon to help raise money for Cancer Research UK, and loved every second of it.
